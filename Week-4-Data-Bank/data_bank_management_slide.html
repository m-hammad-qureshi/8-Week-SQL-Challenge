⚠ Finding 1
Spending Exceeds Deposits
Total customer spending exceeds deposits by ~$24,000 across the 4-month period. This gap suggests customers are withdrawing faster than they are saving — a potential long-term sustainability risk for the bank.
⚠ Finding 2
Low Balance Growth Rate
Only 28.2% of customers increased their closing balance by more than 5% over the period. The remaining 71.8% showed stagnant or declining balances — indicating weak savings behavior across the majority of the customer base.
⚠ Finding 3
50% Negative Closing Balances
Approximately half of all customers ended at least one month with a negative closing balance. This points to overdrafting behavior and lack of financial awareness — requiring proactive intervention from Data Bank.
✓ Recommendation 1
Introduce Compound Interest Rewards
Offer 6% compound interest on customer balances to incentivize saving over spending. Analysis shows compound interest grows ~100x faster than simple interest by month 4 — a compelling reason for customers to maintain higher balances.
✓ Recommendation 2
Extra Data Allocation Rewards
Reward customers who grow their balance by 5%+ monthly with bonus cloud storage allocation. This gamifies saving behavior and directly ties financial health to Data Bank's core product offering — increasing retention.
✓ Recommendation 3
Automated Low Balance Alerts
Trigger automated alerts when customer balance falls below $10. Proactive notification prevents negative balances, improves customer experience, and reduces overdraft risk — building trust in Data Bank's security-first platform.
