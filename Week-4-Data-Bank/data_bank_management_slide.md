# 🏦 Data Bank — Transaction Analysis
**Management Summary — Jan to Apr 2020**

---

### 📊 Key Metrics

*   **Total Transactions**: 5,868
*   **Unique Customers**: 500
*   **Avg Deposit Amount Per Customer**: $2,718
*   **Customers Growing Balance 5%+**: 28.2%
*   **Customers With Negative Balance**: 50%

---

### ⚠️ Key Findings

#### 1. Spending Exceeds Deposits
*   Total customer spending exceeds deposits by **~$24,000** across the 4-month period.
*   This gap suggests customers are withdrawing faster than they are saving, representing a potential long-term sustainability risk for the bank.

#### 2. Low Balance Growth Rate
*   Only **28.2% of customers** increased their closing balance by more than 5% over the period.
*   The remaining **71.8%** showed stagnant or declining balances, indicating weak savings behavior across the majority of the customer base.

#### 3. 50% Negative Closing Balances
*   Approximately **half of all customers** ended at least one month with a negative closing balance.
*   This points to overdrafting behavior and a lack of financial awareness, requiring proactive intervention from Data Bank.

---

### ✓ Strategic Recommendations

#### 1. Introduce Compound Interest Rewards
*   Offer **6% compound interest** on customer balances to incentivize saving over spending.
*   Analysis shows compound interest grows **~100x faster** than simple interest by month 4, providing a compelling reason for customers to maintain higher balances.

#### 2. Extra Data Allocation Rewards
*   Reward customers who grow their balance by **5%+ monthly** with bonus cloud storage allocation.
*   This gamifies saving behavior and directly ties financial health to Data Bank's core product offering, increasing retention.

#### 3. Automated Low Balance Alerts
*   Trigger **automated alerts** when customer balances fall below $10.
*   Proactive notification prevents negative balances, improves customer experience, and reduces overdraft risk.
